## How to Use These Templates

1. **Personalize every send.** Use the placeholders (`{{...}}`) to inject specific details — generic emails get ignored.
2. **Keep subject lines under 60 characters** where possible to avoid truncation.
3. **Send follow-ups only once.** If there’s no reply after the follow-up, move on.
4. **Track in a simple sheet:** site name, contact email, date sent, template used, response status.
5. **Always include a clear ask** and an easy next step (e.g., "Would you be open to a quick pitch?").

## Recommended Sending Setup

- **Tool:** Gmail / Gmass / Apollo / Hunter Campaigns
- **Delay between follow-ups:** 5–7 days
- **Best send days:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM recipient time zone
- **Avoid:** bulk blasting; these work best with careful, low-volume outreach
