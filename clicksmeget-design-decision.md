# ClicksMeGet Design Decision

- Chose dark theme over light theme for young social media users
- Dark mode is default for Gen Z/creators (TikTok, Instagram, Discord, X)
- Dark purple→cyan stands out from typical white/blue SMM panels
- Purple + cyan palette associated with Twitch/Discord/Spotify
- Gradients pop on dark backgrounds, signaling confidence and authenticity
